### **1. Gender Breakdown of Employees**

* Male employees form the majority.
* Female employees follow, with a small number identifying as non-conforming.
  

---

### **2. Race / Ethnicity Breakdown**

* The workforce is predominantly **White**.
* **Native Hawaiian** and **American Indian** groups have the lowest representation.

### **3. Age Distribution**

* Employee age ranges from **20 to 57 years**.
* Largest groups: **25‚Äì34** and **35‚Äì44** years.

### **4. Headquarters vs Remote Employees**

* Most employees work at **headquarters**.

* Terminated employees had an average tenure of **~7 years**.

---
